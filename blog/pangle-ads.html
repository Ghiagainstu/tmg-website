Pangle Ads: ByteDance's Global App Monetization Platform

ByteDance's global app monetization platform powers advertising across 100,000+ apps, delivering 63B daily ad requests to 800M+ active users. Here's what advertisers and developers need to know.

TMG
Tuyue Media Gateway
Paid Media Team · Shanghai

As ByteDance's global app monetization and user growth platform, Pangle powers advertising across hundreds of thousands of apps worldwide, delivering over 63 billion daily ad requests against an audience of 800+ million active users. It is the ad engine behind many of the apps you use every day.

Notably, top-tier advertisers account for 90% of total ad spend on the platform — meaning the inventory quality is high, and the budgets behind it are serious.

🎯 What Pangle Offers Advertisers

Pangle is primarily known as a supply-side platform (SSP) for app publishers, but its advertiser-facing capabilities are substantial:

  • 📦
    Massive, high-quality inventory100,000+ apps across ByteDance's ecosystem and beyond — premium placements you won't find on Western networks.
  • 🎯
    Advanced AI targetingByteDance's recommendation engine, originally built for TikTok and Douyin, now powers cross-app audience matching.
  • 📈
    LTV-optimized biddingBidding models that balance user acquisition cost with Lifetime Value outcomes — not just cheap installs.
  • 🔗
    Exclusive content partnershipsNative ad formats unique to the ByteDance ecosystem — inventory access competitors can't replicate.
  • 📊
    Full-funnel attributionTrack across install, activation, and post-install events — see the complete conversion path.
    • 💡 Global Reach

    Pangle operates globally — not just in China. If you are running mobile user acquisition campaigns across Asia, Latin America, or emerging markets, Pangle's cross-app network gives you access to ByteDance's proprietary audience data across 150+ countries.

    🏆 Standout: Rewarded Video

    Users opt in to watch a full ad in exchange for in-app rewards (coins, lives, premium content). This generates the highest completion rates and the most brand-safe engagement in the mobile ecosystem — particularly valuable for gaming, fintech, and subscription apps.

    📐 Bidding Models

    CPCCost per Click
    CPMCost per 1,000 Impressions
    OCPCOptimized CPC — conversion-based

    The OCPC and OCPM models are particularly valuable for performance advertisers: they use machine learning to optimize bid delivery toward specific conversion events (app installs, in-app purchases, sign-ups) rather than just clicks or impressions.

    🚀 Getting Started on Pangle

    1
    App Developers (Supply Side)
    Open to company entities with an active app. Individual developers not accepted. Integration via SDK. Register at pangle.cn.
    2
    Advertisers (Demand Side)
    Access Pangle's inventory through Ocean Engine's demand-side platform, or through ByteDance's global ad network directly.

    📢 What This Means for Advertisers

    Pangle represents one of the largest and most sophisticated mobile ad ecosystems outside of the Western duopoly (Google/Meta). For international advertisers, it offers distinct advantages:

    Key Takeaway

    Pangle's global SDK network reaches 1.5B+ devices. Rewarded video and interstitial formats deliver highest eCPMs ($10-30). Prioritize video creatives for maximum monetization.

    Pro Tip

    Start with a small test budget and scale based on performance data. Focus on high-intent keywords and audiences first, then expand gradually. Use platform analytics to identify top-performing ad creative and double down on what works.
