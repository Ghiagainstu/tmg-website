Pangle Ads: ByteDance's Global App Monetization Platform

For mobile app developers and advertisers seeking high-quality user acquisition at scale, Pangle (穿山甲) is one of the most powerful platforms you've probably never worked with directly—but almost certainly encountered.

As ByteDance's global app monetization and user growth platform, Pangle powers advertising across hundreds of thousands of apps worldwide, delivering over 63 billion daily ad requests against an audience of 800+ million active users. It is the ad engine behind many of the apps you use every day.

Here is what international media buyers and app developers need to know about Pangle.

The Scale of Pangle

Pangle's numbers are extraordinary by any standard:

800M+ Active Users Reached
63B+ Daily Ad Requests
110B+ Daily Ad Impressions
100K+ Apps Monetized
105K+ Global Advertisers

Notably, top-tier advertisers account for 90% of total ad spend on the platform—meaning the inventory quality is high, and the budgets behind it are serious.

What Pangle Offers Advertisers

Pangle is primarily known as a supply-side platform (SSP) for app publishers, but its advertiser-facing capabilities are substantial. From the advertiser's perspective, Pangle provides:

  • Massive, high-quality inventory from 100,000+ apps across ByteDance's ecosystem and beyond
  • Advanced targeting powered by ByteDance's AI recommendation engine, originally developed for TikTok and Douyin
  • Optimized bidding models that balance user acquisition cost with LTV (Lifetime Value) outcomes
  • Access to exclusive content partnerships and native ad formats unique to the ByteDance ecosystem
  • Full-funnel attribution across install, activation, and post-install events
💡

For international advertisers: Pangle operates globally—not just in China. If you are running mobile user acquisition campaigns across Asia, Latin America, or emerging markets, Pangle's cross-app network gives you access to ByteDance's proprietary audience data across 150+ countries.

Rewarded video is particularly notable for app developers and gaming companies: users opt in to watch a full ad in exchange for in-app rewards (coins, lives, premium content), generating the highest completion rates and brand-safe engagement in the mobile ecosystem.

Bidding Models

Pangle supports the major programmatic pricing models used by sophisticated digital advertisers:

CPC Cost per Click
CPM Cost per Mille (per 1,000 impressions)
OCPC Optimized CPC (conversion-based)
OCPM Optimized CPM (conversion-based)

The OCPC and OCPM models are particularly valuable for performance advertisers: they use machine learning to optimize bid delivery toward specific conversion events (app installs, in-app purchases, sign-ups) rather than just clicks or impressions.

Getting Started on Pangle

For App Developers (Supply Side)

Pangle is open to all company entities with an active app. Individual developers are not accepted. Integration is primarily via SDK. Registration is available at pangle.cn.

For Advertisers (Demand Side)

International advertisers can access Pangle's inventory through Ocean Engine's demand-side platform, or directly through ByteDance's global ad network. For partnership inquiries, contact: union_service@bytedance.com with your company name, primary app, and DAU figures.

What This Means for Advertisers

Pangle represents one of the largest and most sophisticated mobile ad ecosystems outside of the Western duopoly (Google/Meta). For international advertisers, it offers several distinct advantages:

  • Access to ByteDance's proprietary audience data across 800M+ users—data that is not available through Western ad platforms
  • Premium app inventory with 90% of budgets coming from top-tier advertisers ensures a high-quality environment
  • Rewarded video format delivers the highest engagement rates of any in-app format—ideal for gaming, fintech, and subscription apps
  • Global reach across 150+ countries, with strong coverage in Southeast Asia, India, Latin America, and the Middle East
  • OCPC/OCPM optimization means you can bid toward real business outcomes, not just surface-level metrics

Key Takeaways

  • Pangle reaches 800M+ users with 63B daily ad requests and 110B daily impressions—making it one of the top three mobile ad networks globally by volume.
  • Seven ad format types (feed, banner, interstitial, splash, rewarded video, full-screen, immersive) cover every campaign objective from brand awareness to direct response.
  • OCPC and OCPM bidding enable performance-focused campaigns that optimize toward actual conversions, not just clicks.
  • Rewarded video is the standout format for user acquisition in gaming, edtech, and subscription apps—highest completion rate, fully opt-in, premium brand-safe environment.
  • Global reach makes Pangle relevant not just for China campaigns, but for any international mobile marketing strategy targeting emerging markets.
