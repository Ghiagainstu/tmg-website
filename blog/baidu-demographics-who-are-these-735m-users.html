If you're running SEM campaigns in China, you already know Baidu. But do you know who you're reaching? With 735M+ monthly active users and 97.5% coverage of Chinese netizens, Baidu is not just a search engine—it's the gateway to China's digital intent.

We've analyzed the latest data (2025–2026) to help you understand the Baidu user base. Whether you're optimizing for keywords, writing ad copy, or selecting audiences, these demographics will shape your strategy.

Why This Matters

Baidu demographics data reveals the real China digital landscape. Understanding who uses Baidu and how they search is the foundation of any successful China marketing strategy.

📈 Scale Snapshot: 735M+ Users

Baidu's Q2 2025 financial report revealed 735 million monthly active users across its app and search properties—a 5% year-over-year growth. That's not just big; that's 9 out of 10 Chinese internet users.

⚠️ Data Transparency

Baidu does not disclose DAU in its official financial reports. The 300M+ DAU figure is a third-party estimate. Always verify with Baidu's official investor relations page for the latest numbers.

👥 Age Distribution: 80s & 90s Dominate

The core of Baidu's user base is 25–45 years old, representing 62% of the total user base. This is the decision-making generation—people who buy cars, homes, insurance, and enterprise software.

What this means for advertisers: If you're targeting enterprise decision-makers, technical buyers, or homeowners, Baidu's age distribution aligns with your audience. For Gen Z products (streetwear, energy drinks, mobile games), you'll find them on Bilibili or Xiaohongshu instead.

⚧️ Gender Ratio: Male-Skewed, But Balanced in Key Scenarios

Baidu's overall user base is male-skewed at roughly 6:5 (male:female). But this varies by product and search scenario.

💡 Pro Tip

Baidu has never officially released platform-wide gender ratios. The 6:5 ratio is a historical third-party estimate. For your specific keywords, use Baidu Index to see the actual gender split of people searching for your product.

📢 What This Means for Your SEM Strategy

After analyzing these demographics, here's what we tell our clients:

✅ Baidu is for high-intent, decision-stage audiences

With 735M+ users and 6 billion daily searches, Baidu captures active intent. People don't scroll Baidu for fun—they search because they have a problem to solve. That's where your ad needs to be.

✅ Target by city tier + age for B2B

If you're selling industrial equipment, software, or professional services, Baidu's 25–45 urban audience is your sweet spot. Layer city tier + age in your Baidu ad targeting.

⚠️ Don't rely on platform-wide demographics for keyword targeting

The most reliable method is using Baidu Index to check the actual demographics of people searching for your keywords. The person searching "MCU pricing" is different from the person searching "short video drama."
