Creator Search Strategy on Bilibili: KOL-Driven Discovery

On Bilibili, creators — known as UP主 (UP zhu) — are the engine of content discovery. Their videos, articles, and live streams are not only consumed by followers but are also indexed and surfaced by Bilibili's search algorithm, making creator partnerships a powerful and often underutilized lever for search visibility. In 2026, brands that integrate KOL collaboration into their search strategy can achieve sustained organic reach that paid advertising alone cannot replicate.

Bilibili's search engine operates differently from traditional web search. Rather than indexing static web pages, it ranks video content, article posts, and live stream archives based on a combination of relevance signals, engagement metrics, and content quality scores. When a user searches for a topic — whether it is "best budget smartphones," "skincare routine for oily skin," or "new gacha game review" — Bilibili's algorithm evaluates which creator videos best match the query intent.

Several factors determine how creator content ranks in Bilibili search results:

  • Title and tag relevance: Videos with titles and tags that closely match the user's search query receive priority placement.
  • Engagement velocity: Content that accumulates views, likes, coins, shares, and danmaku comments quickly after publication is boosted in search rankings.
  • Creator authority score: Bilibili assigns each UP主 an internal authority score based on their follower count, historical engagement rates, content consistency, and community standing. Higher authority scores translate to better search visibility.
  • Content freshness: Recent uploads are favored for trending and time-sensitive queries, while established evergreen content maintains steady rankings for broader terms.

TMG's research across 8,500 creator videos shows that content ranking in the top five Bilibili search results receives an average of 11.6 times more views than content on the second page of results. This concentration of attention makes search ranking a critical factor in the ROI of any KOL partnership.

🤝 The Role of KOL Partnerships in Search SEO

Traditional influencer marketing on Bilibili focuses on reach and engagement during the campaign period. However, a search-optimized KOL strategy extends the value of every collaboration far beyond the initial posting window. When creator content is strategically designed to rank for target search queries, it continues to generate brand impressions and drive consideration for months — sometimes years — after publication.

Keyword-Aligned Content Briefs

The foundation of a search-optimized KOL campaign is the content brief. Rather than providing creators with generic messaging guidelines, brands should collaborate with KOLs to develop content that naturally incorporates high-volume search keywords into the video title, description, tags, and spoken content.

For example, a skincare brand targeting the keyword "敏感肌 スキンケア" (sensitive skin skincare) should work with beauty UP主 to create a video titled "敏感肌でも使える！2026年おすすめスキンケア5選" (Usable even for sensitive skin! Top 5 recommended skincare products for 2026). This approach ensures the content aligns with actual user search queries while maintaining the creator's authentic voice.

TMG analysis shows that KOL videos produced with search-aligned briefs achieve 67 percent higher first-week organic search impressions compared to videos produced with standard creative briefs.

Content Format Optimization

Not all video formats perform equally in Bilibili search. TMG's data reveals that certain content types consistently outperform others for search visibility:

  • Review and comparison videos: These rank highest for product-related queries, as they match the informational intent of users conducting research before purchase.
  • Tutorial and how-to videos: These perform strongly for problem-solving queries, capturing users who are searching for solutions rather than specific products.
  • List and ranking videos: Titles containing numbers (such as "Top 10" or "5 Best") receive 23 percent higher click-through rates in search results compared to non-numbered titles.
  • Long-form deep dives: Videos over 10 minutes tend to accumulate higher watch time signals, which Bilibili's algorithm interprets as a quality indicator for search ranking.

Multi-Creator Search Saturation

For brands seeking to dominate search results for competitive keywords, a single KOL partnership is rarely sufficient. TMG recommends a multi-creator approach that deploys three to five UP主 to create complementary content targeting the same or related search queries. This strategy, which we call "search saturation," ensures that when a user searches for a target keyword, they encounter multiple brand-affiliated videos across the results page.

Our data shows that brands implementing search saturation strategies see an average 3.4 times increase in branded search query volume within 90 days of campaign launch, compared to brands using single-creator approaches.

🎨 Creator Collaboration Models for Search Performance

Bilibili offers several collaboration models that brands can leverage for search-optimized KOL campaigns. Each model has distinct advantages depending on campaign objectives and budget.

Sponsored Video Partnerships

The most common model involves paying a creator to produce a dedicated video featuring the brand's product or message. For search optimization, TMG recommends specifying target keywords in the content brief and requesting that the creator include these terms in the video title, description tags, and the opening 60 seconds of spoken content, which carries the most weight for Bilibili's search algorithm.

Series-Based Collaborations

Rather than one-off sponsored videos, series-based collaborations involve a creator producing a sequence of three to five videos on a related topic over several weeks. This approach generates multiple indexed assets for the same search queries and signals to Bilibili's algorithm that the creator is producing sustained, authoritative content on the topic. TMG data indicates that series-based collaborations deliver 2.1 times the cumulative search impressions of equivalent single-video campaigns.

Creator-Led Live Search Events

In 2026, Bilibili has expanded its live search features, allowing creators to host live streams that are indexed and surfaced in real-time search results. Brands can sponsor these live events, which generate immediate search visibility while also creating archived content that continues to rank in search long after the live stream ends.

Affiliate and Commission Models

For e-commerce-focused brands, Bilibili's affiliate model allows creators to earn commissions on sales generated through their content. When paired with search optimization, this model incentivizes creators to produce content that ranks for high-converting keywords, aligning the creator's revenue goals with the brand's search visibility objectives.

📈 Measuring KOL Search Impact

Evaluating the search performance of KOL partnerships requires metrics that go beyond traditional influencer KPIs. TMG recommends tracking:

  • Search impression share: What percentage of total impressions for target keywords does KOL-generated content capture?
  • Search ranking longevity: How long does creator content maintain a top-10 search position after publication?
  • Search-driven view attribution: How many views on KOL content originate from Bilibili search rather than from follower feeds or recommendations?
  • Branded search query lift: Does the KOL campaign correlate with an increase in branded search queries on Bilibili?
  • Cross-content halo effect: Does improved search visibility for one KOL video drive higher views and engagement on other brand-related content?

🚀 TMG's Creator Search Strategy Playbook

TMG has developed a systematic approach to integrating KOL partnerships into Bilibili search strategy. Our playbook covers:

  1. Search opportunity mapping: Identify high-volume, low-competition search queries relevant to the brand's category.
  2. Creator selection by search affinity: Choose UP主 whose existing content already ranks for related queries, giving the brand a head start in search visibility.
  3. Search-optimized content briefs: Develop detailed briefs that balance creative authenticity with keyword targeting.
  4. Coordinated multi-creator deployment: Execute search saturation campaigns with three to five creators publishing within a compressed timeline.
