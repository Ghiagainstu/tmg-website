Baidu SEM FAQ
アカウントと設定
アカウント・設定 4 questions
B1 中国国内の企業がなくてもBaiduアカウントを開設できますか？

はい。Baiduは外国企業・団体のアカウント開設に対応しています。中国国内の営業許可証は不要です。手続きは当社が代行します。

B2 外国企業用と中国国内企業用のBaiduアカウントに違いはありますか？

機能的な違いはありません。唯一の違いは広告表示です。広告下部に表示される会社名が、アカウント登録主体に応じて外国企業名または中国企業名になります。

B3 Baiduアカウント開設に必要な書類は何ですか？

必要書類は以下の通りです：

（a）営業許可証コピー＋公証翻訳（会社印付き）;

(b) 政府ウェブサイトの企業情報スクリーンショット＋認証翻訳（会社印付き）；

（c）銀行口座証明書＋指定機関の翻訳文書 (stamped with company seal), such as bank transfer records, bank statements, local tax certificates, checks, or monthly statements;

（d、任意）ランディングページのスクリーンショット（会社印付き）.

これらの書類の準備が難しい場合は、喜んでサポートいたします。

B4 Baidu広告が審査に通らなかった場合はどうすればいいですか？

以下の手順に従ってください：（1）審査結果をよく読む（2）指摘された内容を修正（3）再提出（4）却下が不当と判断した場合は特別審査を申請。管理クライアントの場合は当社が対応します。

B6 Baiduの最低チャージ金額はいくらですか？

Baidu\'s official rule is a minimum top-up of CNY 2,400. However, to save on transaction fees and reduce transfer time, we recommend a single top-up of CNY 10,000 or more.

B7 Baiduアカウント開設にかかる費用は？

プリペイド広告費：最低2,400元。サービス料：100ドル〜（書類翻訳、審査、提出代行を含む）。必要書類の準備が難しい場合は、喜んでサポートいたします。

請求・CPC 3 questions
B8 Baiduは後払いに対応していますか？

いいえ。Baiduはプリペイド方式のみです。広告配信前にアカウントに十分な残高が必要です。掛けや後払いオプションはありません。

B9 Baidu広告の課金方法は？

クリック課金（CPC）： You are charged only when a user clicks your ad. The approximate formula is: your cost per click = (competitor\'s bid x competitor\'s quality score / your quality score) + CNY 0.01. You can also use oCPC (optimized CPC), where you pay for completed conversions rather than clicks.

B10 Baiduのクリック単価は？最低月予算は？

クリック単価：一律の価格はありません。競争の少ないキーワードは1クリック数元程度。競争の激しい業界では、人気キーワードは数十元から100元以上になることもあります。

最低月予算：最低日予算は50元のため、理論上の月額最低額は1,500元です。実際の費用はターゲットキーワードの検索ボリュームによります。上限はありません。

B11 Baiduアカウントが「審査不合格」の場合、広告は配信できますか？

いいえ。審査通過まで広告は配信できません。不合格理由を解決し、承認を得る必要があります。

B12 Baidu広告が否認されるよくある理由は？

最も一般的な否認理由：(1) 期限切れの資格またはライセンス；(2) 禁止用語を含むコンテンツ；(3) Website is unreachable or offline; (4) Content is false, exaggerated, or misleading. Any other compliance violations can also lead to rejection.

B13 Baidu広告でよくある禁止用語は？

よくある禁止用語には以下のような絶対的表現が含まれます： cheapest, number one in the country, 100% effective, guaranteed results, and similar superlative or unverifiable claims. Use Baidu\'s prohibited word detection tool and refer to China\'s Advertising Law for self-inspection before submitting.

B14 医療・金融業界はBaiduで特別な要件がありますか？

はい。医療や金融などの規制業界は、業界固有のライセンスを提出し、効能主張を避けin all ad copy and landing page content, and ensure all claims are substantiated and compliant with Chinese advertising regulations.

At minimum, you need a Chinese business license (if registering directly) or a partnership agreement with a certified agency. Depending on your industry, you may also need:

X3 小紅書のアカウント承認にはどのくらい時間がかかりますか？

The review process on 小紅書（RED）\'s professional account takes up to 30 days. The platform charges a one-time review fee of CNY 600 per application — this is non-refundable even if the review fails. Because of this cost and timeline, it is worth getting the paperwork right the first time.

X4 小紅書のアカウント名のルールは？

The account name must relate to your registered business name, trademark, or website/app name. It cannot be vague, impersonate real people, or mimic 小紅書（RED） itself. Keep it under 12 Chinese characters. Absolute claims like "No. 1," "top-tier," or "best in the country" are prohibited under China\'s Advertising Law and will cause rejection. Pinyin used to replace Chinese characters is not supported.

If your site is on an overseas server, your application will be rejected — you need a China-hosted landing page to proceed.

アバターは中国法に準拠する必要があります：政治的、暴力的、差別的なコンテンツ、禁止業種に関連するイメージは不可。 Once the review passes, you cannot reapply to change the avatar for 7 days.

料金と予算
料金・予算 4 questions
W3 WeChat広告の課金方法は？

WeChatは4つの料金モデルを提供。キャンペーン目標に基づいて選択：

CPM (Cost Per Mille) — 1,000インプレッションごとに課金。リーチがクリックより重要なブランド認知キャンペーンに最適。

CPC (Cost Per Click) — Pay only when someone clicks your ad. Better for driving traffic to your landing page, mini program, or official account.

Per Article — インフルエンサーコンテンツ（相互選択広告）に使用。公開記事ごとに固定料金。

CPS (Cost Per Sale) — 販売が成立した場合のみ課金。コミッション率を設定し、実際の売上から支払い。

Most advertisers start with CPM or CPC. The right choice depends on your campaign objective — we can advise on which model fits your goals best.

W11 oCPCとは？いつ使うべき？

oCPC stands for Optimized Cost Per Click. It is an intelligent bidding mode where WeChat optimizes toward your actual conversion goal rather than just clicks.

With regular CPC, you pay for each click regardless of what happens after. With oCPC, you define a conversion event (like a form submission, app install, or purchase), and WeChat's algorithm optimizes delivery toward users most likely to complete that action.

Use oCPC when you want to focus on results, not just traffic. The catch: you need enough conversion data (typically 20+ conversions per week) for the algorithm to learn effectively. If you are starting from scratch, regular CPC or CPM might be better until you have some data to feed the system.

W12 WeChatの広告費のチャージと引き落とし方法は？

WeChat広告はプリペイドモデル。広告配信前に広告アカウントに資金を入金します。 There is no post-pay or credit option for most advertisers.

Recharge is done through the Tencent Ads platform (ads.e.qq.com) or through your agency partner. Minimum recharge amounts vary — check with us for current thresholds.

Fees are deducted based on your chosen billing model (CPM, CPC, oCPC, etc.) and are calculated in real time. You can set daily or campaign-level spending caps to control your budget, which is useful when you are testing.

For foreign companies, working through a certified agency is usually the smoother option — the agency handles account funding and billing logistics in CNY.

W4 WeChatで広告を出す最低予算は？

There is no official minimum budget published by WeChat, but in practice you need at least CNY 5,000 to 10,000 for meaningful testing. Spending less often means not gathering enough data to optimize your campaigns effectively.

WeChat ads work well for brands that want to build awareness within specific Moments feed placements or target Official Account followers. If your goal is lead generation, there are more cost-efficient platforms — but WeChat is unmatched for reach within the WeChat ecosystem.

請求とCPC
請求・CPC 6 questions
W13 CPM、CPC、CPA、CPTとは？

「何に対して支払うか」と考えてください：

CPM — Cost Per Mille（CPM）。1,000インプレッションごとに課金。1,000人に広告が表示されたら1回課金。

CPC — Cost Per Click（CPC）。クリックされた場合のみ課金。クリックなし、課金なし。

CPA — Cost Per Action（CPA）。アプリのダウンロードやサインアップなど、特定のアクションが完了した場合に課金。 Useful when you want to pay for results, not just traffic.

CPT — Cost Per Time. You pay a fixed fee for a time slot, such as CNY 10,000 for one day. Common for reserved placements and contract deals.

oCPM / oCPC — The smart versions. The system optimizes delivery toward users most likely to convert, which means less guesswork for you. Good for beginners who want the algorithm to handle targeting.

Different ad placements and campaign goals call for different billing models. You pick based on what you are trying to achieve.

W14 オークション広告と契約広告の違いは？

Two different ways to buy ad space:

Auction ads — Compete in real time with other advertisers. Higher bid plus better ad quality wins the slot. You control the budget yourself, can pause anytime, and there is no minimum spend. This is what most advertisers start with.

Contract ads — Negotiate a fixed price with the platform upfront for guaranteed placement. You get a fixed position at a fixed time for a fixed duration. Requires more budget and advance planning. Makes sense when you have a product launch or seasonal campaign and need guaranteed visibility.

Auction ads are simpler and more flexible. Contract ads are for when you need certainty. Most TMG clients begin with auction ads.

W5 WeChatの広告入札の仕組みは？

広告が表示されるかどうかを決定する2つの要素：入札額と広告品質スコア。

Bid — アクション（クリック、インプレッション、コンバージョン）ごとに支払う意思のある金額。キャンペーン作成時に設定。

Ad quality — 関連性、クリック率、ランディングページエクスペリエンスから計算。高品質な広告は低い入札額でもオークションに勝利可能。

It is not just about how much you spend. A well-crafted ad with strong relevance can outperform a competitor bidding higher. WeChat's auction system rewards relevance alongside budget.

W6 WeChatは不正クリックにどう対応していますか？

WeChat runs real-time monitoring for suspicious click activity. If the same user clicks your ad multiple times in a short period, those extra clicks are filtered out and you are not charged for them.

The platform automatically flags patterns like rapid repeated clicks, automated bots, or other suspicious behavior. WeChat will not charge you for clicks deemed fraudulent.

If you notice unusual activity in your dashboard, you can report it. Their team reviews reported cases and adjusts the billing accordingly.

W7 WeChat広告の平均CPCはいくらですか？

There is no fixed CPC — it varies by industry, targeting, and ad placement. WeChat Moments ads tend to have higher CPCs than Mini Program or Official Account placements because of the premium placement.

Low-competition niches might see CPCs of a few yuan. Competitive categories like beauty, fashion, or food and beverage can go significantly higher. The auction-based system means your bid and ad quality both matter.

We will share benchmark data for your specific industry during our onboarding call.

GEOの基礎
GEOの基礎 3 questions
G1 GEOとは具体的に何ですか？

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It is the practice of making your brand and content easier for AI systems — like DeepSeek, Doubao, Kimi, and Wenxin — to find, understand, and reference when answering user questions.

SEOが検索エンジンのランキングを対象とするのに対し、GEOはAI生成の回答を対象とします。 When someone asks an AI a question and the AI mentions your brand in its response, that is GEO working.

G2 GEOとSEOの違いは？

SEOとGEOは異なるチャネルにサービスを提供し、異なる結果を生み出します。

SEOはウェブサイトを検索エンジン結果ページで上位に表示。ユーザーはサイトへのクリックが必要。

GEO aims to get your brand mentioned directly inside an AI answer. The user may never leave the AI interface — they get a recommendation without clicking anything.

G6 GEOのROIはどう測定しますか？

Unlike SEO, where rankings give you a clear number, GEO is harder to measure because AI platforms do not publish their reference sources.

We measure what we can: (1) Share of voice — how often your brand appears in AI responses to questions in your category, tracked over time; (2) Intent match — whether the queries triggering your brand mentions align with your actual target customers; (3) Zero-click reach — estimated impressions from AI answers without clicks.

We provide monthly reports with trend charts. You should expect 3 to 6 months of data before seeing consistent patterns.

G7 GEOで結果が出るまでどのくらいかかりますか？

GEO moves slower than paid ads. Most clients start seeing measurable changes within 3 to 6 months of consistent optimization work.

The reason: AI systems update their models periodically. Changes we make today may take weeks or months to propagate through model retraining cycles. This is different from SEO, where algorithm updates can produce overnight shifts.

We will share early signals within the first month — internal testing results, preliminary probe checks — but the real picture takes patience.

G9 GEOに中国のWebサイトは必要ですか？

Not necessarily, but it helps. AI systems do reference content from global websites — including English-language sites — when answering questions from Chinese users.

That said, a Chinese-language website (or a properly configured subdomain) gives AI systems more signals to work with. It also signals local relevance, which some AI platforms weight higher.

If you do not have a Chinese website yet, we can work with your existing site and recommend whether a Chinese version would be worth the investment for your specific situation.

G10 GEOコンテンツはどのくらいの頻度で更新すべきですか？

3〜6ヶ月ごとにコンテンツを見直し、更新することを推奨。 The AI landscape changes quickly — new model versions, updated training data cutoffs, and shifting user query patterns all affect what gets referenced.

Quarterly refreshes are standard for most clients. If your industry is fast-moving (tech, finance, health), you may want more frequent updates.
